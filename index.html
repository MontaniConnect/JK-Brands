Frequently Asked Questions

These FAQs aims to provide general information about JK Brands products . For personalized advice or specific health concerns, please consult with your healthcare provider.

Are food supplements suitable for children, pregnant women, and lactating mothers?

Our food supplements are suitable for individuals aged 12 years old and above. However, it is important to note that pregnant and lactating mothers are advised not to consume these supplements for their safety.

When is the best time to take the food supplements?

It is recommended to take the supplements before meals or on an empty stomach for optimal absorption.

Can they be taken with other maintenance medications?

Yes, our supplements can be safely taken with other synthetic or herbal supplements without causing harm.

What is the recommended dosage for individuals with existing diseases or ailments?

For those with existing ailments, we recommend taking 2 capsules three times a day for 14 days. After this initial period, it is advised to switch to a maintenance dosage of 2 capsules once a day. It's essential to consult a healthcare professional for personalized guidance.

What is the recommended dosage for preventive or daily use of the food supplements?

For preventive or daily use, we recommend taking 2 capsules once a day before meals.
